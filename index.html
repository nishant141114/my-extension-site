üìñ Quick Buying Guide: How to Choose the Best Earphones

  • Sound Quality: Look for balanced audio with clear bass, mids, and highs. Check for driver size (10mm+ is good for deeper bass).
  • Battery Life: For wireless models, aim for at least 8-10 hours of playback on a single charge.
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.0 or higher ensures stable connection and better battery efficiency.
  • Comfort & Fit: Lightweight designs with multiple ear tip sizes ensure comfortable long-term wear.
  • Build Quality: Water/sweat resistance (IPX4 or higher) is essential for gym use or outdoor activities.
  • Brand Reputation: Stick to trusted brands like Sony, OnePlus, boAt, JBL, or Realme for better after-sales support.
