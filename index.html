Product Finder Extension

About This Extension

Product Finder is a browser extension that helps users quickly search for Amazon products by product title and view real-time prices.

How It Works

- Enter a product title in the extension
- Instantly get matching Amazon products
- Compare prices before buying

Why Use It?

Save time, discover products faster, and never miss a great deal on Amazon.
